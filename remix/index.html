How to Play

1

Jump

Tap for a normal jump. Hold briefly for extra height. The collision area is smaller than Rex's artwork.

2

Obstacles

Sleeping cats and ducks are low. Stumps and pizza are medium. Trees require an early, held jump.

3

Power Orbs

Blue grants a shield. Green grants laser shots. Gold charges Mega Roar.

4

Five Lives

After a hit, Rex flashes and receives two seconds of protection so one mistake does not remove several lives.
